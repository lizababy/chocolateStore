About Our Chocolate Company

Made in San Diego Since ’83

San Diego is more than an address for this chocolate company. It's home — where we live, who we are, and how we do business. And from the first truffle in 1983 up to the present day, San Diego has inspired us to take a craftsman’s approach to chocolate: creativity, patience, and mastery.

To Bring You The Best

That's the mission — it’s why we show up every day. To make chocolates that amaze with fresh ingredients and exquisite flavor. A few things have changed over 30 years, to be sure. What started with chocolate truffles has grown to include hot chocolate gifts, organic chocolate, sea salt caramels, and other tempting treats.
